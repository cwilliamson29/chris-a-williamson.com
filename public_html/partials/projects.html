Ulbora CMS 2.0 is the first Open Source MEAN stack CMS and blog platform. Ulbora CMS 2.0 is built with MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS and Node.js. Ulbora CMS 2.0 is a Cloud ready CMS and blog platform with switchable templates. Templates can be written in either AngularJS or with conventional server-side template engines like EJS or Jade. Templates can even use a combination of both Angular and server-side template technology.
